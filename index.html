The Problem We Set Out to Solve

Every year, thousands of world-class events take place across the globe — yet discovering them requires crawling through dozens of fragmented websites, Twitter threads, and email newsletters. Founders miss investment summits. Developers skip conferences that could have changed their trajectory. Investors overlook policy forums shaping the very markets they operate in. EventXed eliminates that friction entirely.

Built by Professionals, for Professionals

EventXed emerged from a shared frustration within our founding team — all veterans of the global tech and fintech ecosystem. Having attended hundreds of events across four continents, we understood precisely how much value was locked inside these gatherings: deal flow, partnerships, mentorship, and insights that simply don't exist anywhere else.

We designed EventXed to serve five core communities: technology professionals tracking the latest developments in AI, Web3, and cloud infrastructure; fintech innovators navigating regulation and scaling payment systems; startup founders raising capital and finding product-market fit; investors deploying capital into the next generation of companies; and government and policy leaders shaping the digital economy from the top down.

Our curation is rigorous. Every event on EventXed is manually reviewed for legitimacy, relevance, and quality before it appears on the platform. We maintain direct relationships with organizers on six continents. We are not an aggregator — we are a curator.

Our Core Values

Integrity First

Every event listing is manually verified. We never accept payment to elevate events in our rankings. Our curation is independent and honest.

Truly Global

We actively resist the bias toward Western tech hubs. Events in Lagos, Nairobi, Riyadh, and Singapore receive the same prominence as those in San Francisco and London.

Community-Driven

Our platform is shaped by the professionals who use it. We actively solicit event submissions, feedback, and category suggestions from our user base.

Always Current

Upcoming events are always surfaced first. Past events are preserved as a research archive, because context and precedent matter in every field.

Accessibility

We surface free and low-cost events alongside premium conferences, because great ideas and great communities exist at every price point.

Design Excellence

Discovery should be effortless and beautiful. We invest heavily in design because finding your next event should be as good as the event itself.
